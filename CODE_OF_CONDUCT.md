# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Accepting criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the project
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code. Repeated violations may result in a ban.

## Reporting

Report issues to the maintainers via GitHub Issues or email. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
